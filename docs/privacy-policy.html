Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

zenflo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

The Short Version

zenflo is a fully offline productivity app. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts. Your data lives exclusively on your device in a local database. We cannot see it, and we never will.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and AsyncStorage:

  • Goals, habits, and tasks
  • Focus sessions and completion history
  • XP, levels, badges, and streak data
  • Flight log (weekly review) entries
  • Categories (Work, Health, Personal, Hobby, and any custom categories you create)
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • iOS widget data (shared between the app and widget extension via App Groups — stays on device)
  • Your name (used only for the greeting)
  • Notification and calendar preferences

This data never leaves your device. There is no cloud sync, no remote server, and no backup service operated by us.

iOS Widgets

zenflo offers home screen widgets that display your habits, tasks, and progress at a glance. To power these widgets, a small subset of your data is shared between the main app and the widget extension using App Groups — a secure, on-device data-sharing mechanism provided by Apple.

This data is stored in a shared container on your device only. No widget data is transmitted over the network or sent to any server. The widget extension reads data locally, renders it on your home screen, and that is the full extent of the process.

Data Collection

zenflo collects zero data from you. Specifically:

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reporting sent to us
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No cookies or web tracking

Device Permissions

zenflo may request the following permissions, all of which are optional:

  • Calendar access — To sync your tasks and events with Apple Calendar or Google Calendar on your device. Calendar data is read and written locally through the operating system's calendar API. No calendar data is transmitted anywhere.
  • Notifications — To send local reminders (morning ritual, streak alerts, flight log). These are scheduled entirely on-device. No push notification servers are involved.

You can deny or revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings without affecting core app functionality.

Third-Party Services

zenflo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services. The app makes no network requests.

Data Export & Deletion

You are in full control of your data:

  • Export — You can export all your data as JSON from Profile > Data > Export as JSON.
  • Delete — You can permanently delete all data from Profile > Data > Clear all data. This action is irreversible.
  • Uninstall — Removing the app from your device deletes all zenflo data permanently.

Children's Privacy

zenflo does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of children's data being processed.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and the effective date above. Since zenflo collects no data, any future changes would only occur if we added a feature that changes this (in which case we would ask for your explicit consent first).
